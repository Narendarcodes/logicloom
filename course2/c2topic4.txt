    <h1>Integer Data Type :</h1>
    <ul class="custom-list">
      <p> Commonly used in enterprise-level applications, Android app development, and large-scale systems due to its portability and scalability.</p>
     <li><b>Range :</b>-2,147,483,648 to 2,147,483,647 </li>
      <li><b>Size :</b>4 bytes</li>
      <li><b>Format Specifier :</b>%d</li> 
    </ul>

    <!--for another data type-->
    <h1>Character Data Type :</h1>
    <ul class="custom-list">
      <p> Commonly used in Text processing,User Interface,File operation,Networking,Symbolic Representation</p>
     <li><b>Range :</b>(-128 to 127) or (0 to 255) </li>
      <li><b>Size :</b>1 byte</li>
      <li><b>Size :</b>%c</li> 
    </ul>

    <h1>Float Data Type :</h1>
    <ul class="custom-list">
      <p> Commonly used in Financial Calculations,Scientific and Engineering Calculations,Graphics and Computer Vision,Statical Analysis</p>
     <li><b>Range :</b>1.2E-38 to 3.4E+38 </li>
      <li><b>Size :</b>4 bytes</li>
      <li><b>Size :</b>%f</li> 
    </ul>

    <h1>Double Data Type :</h1>
    <ul class="custom-list">
      <p> Commonly used in Complex Calculations in mathmatics,Telecommunication,Space Exploration,Database operation</p>
     <li><b>Range :</b>1.7E-308 to 1.7E+308</li>
      <li><b>Size :</b>8 bytes</li>
      <li><b>Size :</b>%lf</li> 
    </ul>
