Different Steps Involved in Problem Solving

Algorithm :

  • Identify the Problem: Clearly define and understand the issue at hand.
  • Analyze the Situation: Break down the problem, gather relevant information, and examine potential factors.
  • Generate Solutions: Brainstorm and come up with various possible solutions to the problem.
  • Select the Best Solution:Evaluate the pros and cons of each solution and choose the most effective one.
  • Implement and Evaluate: Put the chosen solution into action and assess its effectiveness. Make adjustments if necessary.
