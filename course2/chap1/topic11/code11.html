High level and low level language

High Level Language

  • A high-level language is any programming language, which is understandable to the user.
  • It enables development of a program.
  • High-level languages are user-friendly to programmers. They’re also simple to develop and debug.
  • It is simple to learn.
  • For translation requires a complier or interpreter.
  • Ex- C,C++,Java etc

Low Level Language

  • A low-level language is a programming language, which is not easily understandable to the user.
  • It is very close to writing actual machine instructions.
  • It deals with a computer's hardware components.
  • They’re complex to develop and debug.
  • For translation requires an assembler.
  • Ex- Machine code, Assembly code.
